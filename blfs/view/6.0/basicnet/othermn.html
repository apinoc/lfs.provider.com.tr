Other Mail and News programs

Pan-0.14.2 is a GTK2 based newsreader program.

knode is a Qt based newsreader program from kdepim-3.3.2.

kmail is a Qt based mail client from kdepim-3.3.2.

Balsa-2.2.6 is a GTK2 based mail client.

Mozilla-1.7.5 includes both a mail client and newsreader in its installation.

Thunderbird-1.0 is a mail/news client based on the Mozilla code base.

Evolution-2.0.2 includes a GTK2 based mail client.
