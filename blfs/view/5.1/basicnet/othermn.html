Other Mail and News programs

Pan-0.14.2 is a GTK2 based newsreader program.

KNode is a Qt based newsreader program from the section called “kdepim-3.2.2”.

KMail is a Qt based mail client from the section called “kdepim-3.2.2”.

Balsa-2.0.15 is a GTK2 based mail client.

Mozilla-1.6 includes both a mail client and newsreader in its installation.

MozillaThunderbird-0.5 is a mail/news client based on the Mozilla code base.

Evolution-1.4.5 includes a GTK2 based mail client.
